Polar Park

Gérald Hustache-Mathieu

Thriller comedy, French language, returnable – 6x52min – Release Fall 2023
Nominated at Séries Mania 2023

Synopsis

Coming back to his mother's hometown for inspiration, a detective novel writer finds himself investigating the trail of a most artistic serial killer. Starring French movie star Jean-Paul Rouve (>100M tickets in career), Polar Park is a thrilling, offbeat, cleverly crafted serial-killer series that mixes like no other noir elements with comic touches.​
