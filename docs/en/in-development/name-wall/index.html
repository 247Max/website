The Name On The Wall

Hervé le Tellier

Documentary, History – 90min – In development

Synopsis

«The Name on the Wall». «The Name» is that of André Chaix, a 20 years old FTP resistant mortally wounded by the Germans during their retreat in August 44. « The Wall» is the one from the garden of Hervé Le Tellier’s house near Dieulefit, in Drôme, bought shortly before the pandemic.​

Created by

Hervé Le Tellier and Bernard Faroux, from the novel “The Name On The Wall” by Hervé Le Tellier.
